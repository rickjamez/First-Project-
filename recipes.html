Vegan waffle w/ no oil

Ingredients/Instructions

  • 1 mashed banana
  • 1 1⁄2 - 2 cups whole weat flour
  • 2 tbsp water
  • mix
  • 1 - 2 baking powder
  • 1⁄2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp mexican vanilla extract
  • add 1 1⁄4 almond or coconut milk or to your like of consistency.
  • mix
  • sweetner (recommend agave or coconut sugar) if you like your waffles sweet.
  • mix
  • last add 100% real choclate chips or fruit

  • dont forget to check the ingredients of your choclate and to make sure it has NO DAIRY.

  • use very little coconut oil on your waffle maker so it wont stick and ruin your beautiful waffle.

  • you can also add hemp or flax seeds into the batch if you have any.

  • FOR CHEAP HERBS AND FRUIT GO TO YOUR LOCAL FLEE MARKET!!!
